Who we are

Get to know about VidhyadiRaja Grama Nidhi L.T.D

We are a community-focused financial institution committed to uplifting rural lives through access to affordable credit and sustainable savings options. Our mission is to bridge the gap between financial services and underbanked villages by supporting self-help groups, small businesses, and women entrepreneurs.

With years of trusted service, we are playing a vital role in rural economic development, one family at a time.
